    Chaos Destroyers

    Chaos Destroyers - it's a game strategy. You should defeat monsters in
    Forest of Wind and Win Orcs and Ogres Chief in Castle of Chaos.

    Strategy

    You must use best strategy in battles.
    Or you lost your World.

    Battle

    You can battle with Your friend in 2 players
    mode or play campany.

    Win

    If you win battles you took trophy and more
    gems. But it's not easy...
